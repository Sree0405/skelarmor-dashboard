# SkelArmor — For Gym Owners & Teams

**SkelArmor** is a single place to run your fitness business—from everyday clients and payments to bigger gym projects—without drowning in spreadsheets.

---

## What you get

- **Your clients, organized** — See who is active, who needs a nudge, and what’s happening at a glance.
- **Payments you can trust** — Know what’s paid, what’s due, and where money sits—whether it’s personal training or a major project.
- **Progress that motivates** — Track weight and body metrics over time so clients see real results.
- **Projects under control** — When you’re building or upgrading a space, keep budget, status, and payments in one workflow.

---

## Who it’s for

- **Gym owners and operators** juggling clients, billing, and growth.
- **Franchise or partner gyms** that need a clear view of their projects and payments.
- **Training clients** who want a simple portal for their own progress and history.

---

## Dashboard highlights (owners)

- A **smart overview** that points to who needs attention, what’s growing, and what’s at risk.
- **Client lists** for personal training and for partner gyms.
- **Projects** for fit-outs and major work—budget, timeline, and payments in one place.
- **Leads** (when enabled for your business) so website enquiries become follow-ups, not lost emails.

---

## What your clients see (training login)

- A **clean, focused** space: their overview, progress over time, and payment history—without the noise of a full admin system.

---

## SkelArmor vs typical gym software

Most tools stop at **memberships** or **class booking**. SkelArmor is built for operators who also sell **coaching**, run **complex projects**, and need the **full money story** in one place—without sacrificing a modern, professional experience.

1. Sign in with the access your administrator provides.
2. Land on the dashboard built for **your role**—owner, partner, or client.
3. Use the menu to move between **clients**, **progress**, **projects**, **payments**, and **profile**.
