# 🏋️ Gym Intelligence Platform (SkelArmor)

**One Platform to Run Your Entire Fitness Business**

A full-stack dashboard designed for gym operators to manage clients, track training progress, monitor payments, and scale across multiple branches — all from a single intelligent command center.

---

LIVE PREVIEW : [dashboard](https://dashboard.skelarmor.com/)

## 🚀 Overview

Gym Intelligence Platform (SkelArmor) is a comprehensive SaaS solution built to streamline fitness business operations. It eliminates the need for spreadsheets and disconnected tools by providing a unified system for:

- Gym management
- Personal training tracking
- Franchise operations
- Client engagement
- Payment monitoring

---

## 🧩 Core Modules

### 1. 🏢 Gym Setup & Management
- Digital member onboarding
- Package and subscription management
- Payment tracking with reminders
- Attendance and occupancy monitoring
- Staff roles and permissions

---

### 2. 🌐 Franchise Management
- Multi-branch tracking
- Milestone and project tracking
- Franchise payment scheduling
- Branch-level performance analytics
- Centralized trainer and team control

---

### 3. 💪 Personal Training Clients
- Client transformation tracking
- Body metrics and progress logs
- Session tracking and adherence monitoring
- Payment and renewal status
- Client-facing progress summaries

---

## ⚙️ Platform Features

- 📊 **Progress Analytics**  
  Visual insights into transformation, adherence, and revenue trends.

- 💳 **Payment Tracking**  
  Automated reminders and outstanding balance monitoring.

- 👥 **Client Management**  
  Complete digital profiles with goals, history, and metrics.

- 📈 **Smart Reports**  
  Exportable reports for revenue, attendance, and performance.

- 🔍 **Actionable Insights**  
  Identify at-risk clients, unpaid dues, and slow-performing branches.

- 🏢 **Multi-Branch Control**  
  Manage multiple gym locations from a single dashboard.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. **Set Up Your Business**  
   Configure gym details, packages, and team.

2. **Add Clients & Plans**  
   Onboard members and assign training programs.

3. **Track Everything**  
   Monitor progress, payments, and performance in real time.
